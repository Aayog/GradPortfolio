About Me

Hi, I'm Aayog Koirala, an ambitious software developer currently pursuing my Master's in Computer Science at Miami University, with an expected graduation in May 2024. I am proficient in various programming languages, including C, C++, Python, Java, JavaScript, PHP, and SQL, and have hands-on experience with frameworks and tools like Laravel, Node.js, Flask, Angular, Docker, S3, and Django.

I completed my Bachelor's degree in Computer Science at Miami University in 2022 and have taken a wide range of courses in topics such as Data Structures, Algorithms, Object-Oriented Programming, and Software Engineering. For my capstone project, I led the development of a chatbot for the university library using Google Dialogflow and Node.js.

Throughout my academic journey, I've been actively involved in various projects, including developing a scalable CMS website using Django REST Framework, experimenting with NLP using Spacy, and creating user-friendly interfaces for games and utilities. My Python script, which tracks eye movements for scrolling on social media sites, won an MLH-hosted hackathon at the University of Indianapolis in 2018.

I've also gained valuable real-world experience through multiple roles at Miami University - Myaamia Center, Community Health Network, and Cummins Inc. As a software developer, teaching assistant, healthcare analytics intern, and IT intern, I've honed my technical and non-technical skills, making me an ideal candidate for any software development role.

Feel free to connect with me on LinkedIn or explore my projects on GitHub.
